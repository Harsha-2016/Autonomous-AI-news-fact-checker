India's unemployment rate rose to 8.5% in January 2024 according to the 
Centre for Monitoring Indian Economy. The government disputes these figures, 
citing official Labour Bureau data showing 4.2% unemployment. Experts say 
the methodology differences account for the gap — CMIE uses household surveys 
while the Labour Bureau uses employer reports. Some economists argue that 
gig economy workers are miscounted in both surveys, potentially making the 
true figure anywhere between 6% and 11%.
