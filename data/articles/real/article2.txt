The Reserve Bank of India kept the benchmark repo rate unchanged at 6.5% 
in its February 2024 monetary policy committee meeting, marking the sixth 
consecutive pause. RBI Governor Shaktikanta Das said the decision was 
unanimous among all six committee members. Inflation remains the primary 
concern at 5.1%, still above the 4% target. The RBI revised GDP growth 
forecast for FY2025 to 7%, up from the earlier estimate of 6.5%.
